Welcome to:

Embark on a unique journey in Täpläjahti, where you guide a young fly agaric named Näsi on his quest to find his missing spots. In this engaging traffic game, you'll navigate through challenging intersections based on the real streets of Tampere. Learn traffic rules and dodge various dangers as you aim to collect all the spots needed for Näsi to join the mushroom school.

Features:

  • Unique drawing mechanics: Draw paths to safely guide Näsi through the traffic maps.
  • Seasonal variation: Tackle all 16+2 maps across different seasons: winter, spring, summer, and autumn.
  • Learn traffic rules: An educational approach makes this game both fun and instructive.
  • Natural and aesthetic graphics: Abundant natural elements and creatively designed insect cars enhance your gaming experience.
